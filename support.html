Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a third hierarchy level?
The third level requires a Pro licence. Drag your field into the Level 3 well and activate your licence via Microsoft AppSource.

Why are my chips showing only 20 values?
The Free tier is limited to 20 values per level. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited values.

How do I reset all selections?
Click the ↺ Reset button (Pro) or click the "All" chip to clear all filters.

Does the visual work in Power BI Service?
Yes. ChipSlicer Hierarchy is fully compatible with Power BI Desktop and Power BI Service.
